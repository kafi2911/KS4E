OBC Architecture

  • MCU: PIC18F67J94
  • Watchdog: External Timer
  • Memory: MT25QL Flash

Power (EPS)

  • Output 1: 12V Boost
  • Output 2: 3.3V Buck
  • Protection: OCP (LTC4361)

  • Tranceiver: XBee Series 2
  • Bridge: FTDI USB/UART
  • Mode: TX / CW / RX
